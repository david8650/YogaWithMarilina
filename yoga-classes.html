Yoga Classes

Open Group Classes

My classes are carefully sequenced and alignment-based.
Gently challenging and deeply nourishing. I invite you to listen to your body and move with the breath to flow with ease and grace through the different postures and the challenges life throws at us.

I love movement, I am fascinated by the human body, the breath and its power, the endless potential of the human mind.

I currently lead Hatha Yoga flow group Sessions in Sagres: expect to warm up each part of your body, move around with joy and finish with a meaningful meditation/visualization.

Weekly group classes:

  • Friday 6 pm (GMT +0) - Hatha yoga Flow
  • Sunday 9:30 AM (GMT+0) - Hatha yoga Flow


    • All classes have a duration of 75 minutes

    Price & packages:

    - Drop in class: €10

    - Bundle of 5 classes: €45

    Private Sessions

    A private yoga session it’s an opportunity for us to work together and focus on the unique requirements of your body and energy. It’s a powerful tool to deepen your knowledge, work on yourself and develop the yoga practice that works for you.

  • A purpose built practice for your needs, interests and goals
  • Individual guidance
  • Flexible start times


    • A 1:1 includes:

  • An introductory chat with me to get to know each other and to define what you would like to work on during your private session.
  • 1 hour private yoga tuition with me - online or face to face
  • The recording of your yoga session


    • Price & packages:
